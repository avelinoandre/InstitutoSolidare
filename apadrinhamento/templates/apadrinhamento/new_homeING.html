Solidare Institute is a civil society organization under private law in the social assistance sector. Founded in 2006, it was an initiative of the Baptist Church in Coqueiral and community leaders. Its goal is to promote the social, political, and educational development of children and adolescents regardless of ethnicity or creed, contributing to the formation of citizens aware of their potential as agents of transformation in their social reality.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does it mean to be a sponsor and why is it important? ↓

Being a sponsor is more than just providing financial support to a social program. It's about helping children’s dreams come true and contributing to improving their quality of life, as established by the Child and Adolescent Statute (Law 8.069 of 07/13/1990). It’s embracing the cause of childhood and adolescence and actively participating in the development of these young citizens.

Who are the children and adolescents to be sponsored? ↓

They are children and adolescents enrolled in our social programs who need support to achieve their dreams and improve their living conditions.

How are the donated resources used? ↓

The donations go to the Integral Citizenship Program and help with food security. The children attend after-school activities, where they receive two meals and participate in educational, sports, recreational, and professional training activities.
See our annual reports and financial statements at https://institutosolidare.org.br/transparencia/.

How will I communicate with my sponsored child? ↓

Communication is encouraged and will be mediated by Solidare's team. Letters, reports, and updates will be shared periodically, keeping you informed about your sponsored child’s development—sometimes in real-time.

Protection of vulnerable children and adolescents ↓

We follow all legal child protection guidelines, especially the Child and Adolescent Statute (Law 8.069 of July 13, 1990). We have a Child Protection Policy to guide all our care efforts. If you suspect misconduct, contact us via WhatsApp (81) 3251-4480 or email: denuncia@institutosolidare.org.br.
Read our protection policy at www.institutosolidare.org.br.

What can I do besides financial support? ↓

Beyond donating, you can offer advice, motivational messages, or be a role model. Correspondence should respect child safety principles. If you're a professional or have time for volunteer work, contact our Admin and Finance department at falecom@institutosolidare.org.br with your availability and area of interest.

What should I write in a letter to my sponsored child? ↓

Share a bit about yourself, your family, meaningful experiences, ask questions, offer encouragement—write in simple, friendly language. Creativity is welcome, especially for younger children (e.g., drawings).

Will I receive a reply to my letter? ↓

Yes, your sponsored child will reply to your letters and may even write spontaneously, even if you haven’t sent one.

Can I message my sponsored child on social media? ↓

For safety, all communication must go through the Sponsor Portal or via WhatsApp (81) 3251-4480 and email: apd@institutosolidare.org.br.

How can I update my information in the sponsorship program? ↓

Whenever your phone number, email, or address changes, please update your information via the Sponsor Portal. You can view and edit your data anytime.
